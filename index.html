I have extensive experience in front-end and back-end development, including building custom modules and components with C#, .NET, TypeScript, JavaScript, Angular.js, HTML, CSS, as well as working with MSSQL, T-SQL, PostgreSQL, and Knime for reporting, data analysis, and database management. I also have experience with Metabase, Docker, and other modern development tools. I am passionate about creating innovative applications, features, and tools, and I thrive in solving complex problems, optimizing performance, and delivering scalable enterprise solutions. Continuous learning, self-improvement, and mastering new technologies define my professional approach.
